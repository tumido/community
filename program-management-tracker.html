Operate First community plan status#

The status table has columns with the following meanings:

  • Workstream: the interdependency being tracked, linking back to related issues on the community board.

  • Status color: At a glance indication of the status of the workstream:

    • Workstream is in trouble, somehow seriously off-track, or significantly behind schedule

    • There is an increased risk of the workstream milestones being affected.

    • When everything is going well but you have some reservations about calling it all the way Green.

    • The project is on-track and on-schedule.

  • Notes: Details about the status, listing blockers and actions.
